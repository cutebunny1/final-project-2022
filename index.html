Vegetarian Food Ideas!

Hi! My name is Kripa and I'm a student at the University of Michigan! For a class project, I was tasked with creating a website and I decided to make it about food. Food has always been very special and personal to me, so I hope to share some inspiration of potential meals and desserts with you all.


Something that has always intrigued me is how few vegetarians and vegans there are. I have been vegetarian all my life, so it was not a shift of lifestyle for me. However, it is for a lot of people. I realize part of the barrier to entry to becoming vegetarian is figuring out what to eat. So, I have compiled a list of vegetarian food ideas -- split up by meals and dessert. Hope this helps people meal prep and inspires more people to choose being vegetarian!



Through this website, I hope to show you there is more to being vegetarian than only eating salads, fresh fruits, and vegetables. Though, fresh fruits and vegetables are certainly healthy. I am personally a huge fan of lychees, as you can see on the website icon and in the image above.
